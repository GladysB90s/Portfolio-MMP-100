About Me

Gladys B. an African-American born filmmaker born and raised in Harlem, NY. Fell in love with storytelling at an early age, with Her first collaborations starting in the classroom with fellow students. First writing, and crafting, later performing her own works on stage. She would take a hiatus and returm to not only write, but direct as well.

Often recognized for her hardwork and vision. Starting out at BMCC, with plans to continue her journey with education beyond by moving on to NYU and Columbia. Hoping to graduating with a degree with a concentration in film, and civil enginereering. Gladys B. Focuses on projects that merges real life experiences with visuals. The goal and hope of beiong able to impact the heart enough with film to bring about movement that brings changes to reality.
